Cover art in the visual tradition of the **"For Dummies" book series**.

Style: **bold vector/flat illustration** with thick black linework on a bright
yellow background (#ffde2b). Chunky, friendly, cartoonish but not childish —
think magazine comic panel, not a kids' book. High contrast. Primary colour
palette only: yellow, black, white, with one accent colour permitted (red or
blue).

Subject: a **playful visual metaphor** for **{{TOPIC}}** — a cheerful character
doing/using/pointing at the topic. Triangle-head "Dummies guy" vibe is optional
but encouraged. The image should make someone smile.

Composition: subject centred or right-of-centre. **Leave the left 35% of the
image clear yellow** — the title will be CSS-overlaid there. A big black
triangular wedge will be overlaid at the bottom-right by CSS too, so keep your
subject clear of that corner. No letterforms, numbers, or logos in the image.

Background stays flat yellow (#ffde2b). Accept slight paper texture; reject
gradients, drop-shadow gloss, or photoreal lighting.

The whole thing should feel iconic even as a thumbnail.
